About Us

The Internal Medicine Club at King Saud bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences is dedicated to fostering a community of medical students passionate about internal medicine. We aim to provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for professional development.

Our Members

We are a diverse group of medical students and residents committed to excellence in internal medicine. Our members benefit from networking opportunities, educational resources, and collaborative projects.
